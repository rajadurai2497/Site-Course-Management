MARKETS

STOCKS

Buying a stock means, you’re literally buying a part of that company you’re investing in. Stocks represent the financial trade sector. Lurecap Academy provides deep ideas about trading and investing through its courses.
DERIVATIVES

Futures and options are the major types of stock derivatives traded in a share market. These contracts try to hedge market risks involved in stock market trading by locking in the price beforehand. Lurecap Academy will teach you how to effectively utilise FNO.
COMMODITY

commodities represent the raw products sector. commodities are not physically traded on an exchange and instead traded through commodity futures and forward contracts. Lurecap Academy guides you to trade in commodity market.
FOREX

The foreign exchange market is where currencies are traded. The market is open 24 hours a day, five and a half days a week, and currencies are traded worldwide. Forex market can be extremely active any time of the day. Lurecap Academy provides structured classes to trade in Forex market.
